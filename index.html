Explore Tacoma Neighborhoods

Tacoma is a city of distinct communities, each with its own charm and advantages. Emily’s deep familiarity with the area helps you find a neighborhood that fits your lifestyle.

Proctor District

The Proctor District offers a small‑town feel with boutique shops, the historic Blue Mouse Theatre and a popular farmers’ market. Tree‑lined streets feature historic Craftsman homes with modern updates, and nearby schools such as Washington‑Hoyt Elementary enjoy high ratings.

University Place

Family‑friendly University Place combines suburban comfort with amenities like the waterfront Chambers Bay Golf Course and scenic walking trails. Top-rated schools and a range of housing options make it a versatile choice for many buyers.

Central Tacoma

Central Tacoma blends historic Craftsman homes with newer developments and is close to the University of Puget Sound and Tacoma General Hospital. An emerging cultural scene along 6th Avenue offers restaurants and nightlife options.

McKinley Hill

Known for panoramic views of Mount Rainier and a quiet, community‑focused atmosphere, McKinley Hill features affordable homes with great potential for appreciation. Residents enjoy local coffee shops and green spaces like McKinley Park.

Ruston

Trendy Ruston offers upscale living along Commencement Bay with waterfront trails and scenic views. The Point Ruston development features shops, restaurants and a luxury cinema, while schools such as Sherman Elementary score highly.
